# SparkLabs Cinematic Demo Storyboard

## Overview
This storyboard documents the complete cinematic walkthrough experience for SparkLabs, a production-ready creator platform. The demo showcases five key workflow scenes with world-class animation, avatar-driven narration, and responsive design across all devices.

**Duration**: 30-60 seconds
**Format**: 1080p/60fps (16:9 landscape + 9:16 vertical)
**Style Variants**: Minimalist Flat, Cinematic Realism, Futuristic Neon
**Accessibility**: WCAG AAA compliant with colorblind-safe palettes

## Style Variants

### 1. Minimalist Flat
**Characteristics**:
- Simple solid colors (no gradients)
- Flat shadows (sm only)
- Sans-serif typography
- Ample whitespace
- Clean icons (outline style)
- Neutral color palette (neutral-900, primary-500)

**Color Palette**:
- Background: White / neutral-50
- Text: neutral-900
- Accent: primary-500
- Success: success-500
- Shadows: Minimal (2-4px)

### 2. Cinematic Realism
**Characteristics**:
- Rich gradients
- Deep shadows (xl, 2xl)
- Dramatic lighting
- Photo-realistic textures
- Depth of field blur
- Immersive atmosphere

**Color Palette**:
- Background: Gradient from-primary-600 to-primary-900
- Text: White with subtle glow
- Accents: Gold (#F59E0B) + White
- Shadows: Heavy with blur

### 3. Futuristic Neon
**Characteristics**:
- Bright neon accents
- Dark backgrounds
- Glowing effects
- Geometric shapes
- Cyberpunk aesthetic
- High contrast

**Color Palette**:
- Background: neutral-950 / Black
- Neon: Cyan (#06B6D4) + Magenta (#EC4899)
- Text: White with glow
- Effects: Box-shadow with neon colors
