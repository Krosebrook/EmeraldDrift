# Privacy Policy

**Creator Studio Lite**  
**Last Updated: December 2025**

## Introduction

Creator Studio Lite ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile picture (optional)

### Content Data
- Posts and captions you create
- Media files you upload
- Scheduled content
- Draft content

### Platform Connections
- Social media account usernames
- OAuth tokens for connected platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Pinterest)
- Platform-specific analytics data

### Usage Data
- App interaction patterns (anonymized)
- Feature usage statistics
- Performance metrics

### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Push notification tokens

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Services**: Create, schedule, and publish content to your connected platforms
2. **Display Analytics**: Show performance metrics and engagement data
3. **Enable Scheduling**: Send notifications for scheduled posts
4. **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
5. **Technical Support**: Diagnose issues and provide assistance

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Your data is stored securely on your device using encrypted AsyncStorage
- **Credentials**: Platform OAuth tokens are stored using secure keychain/keystore APIs
- **No Server Storage**: We do not maintain servers that store your personal data
- **Encryption**: All stored data uses industry-standard encryption

## Third-Party Services

When you connect social media platforms, we access their APIs using OAuth 2.0 authentication. We request only the permissions necessary to:

- Post content on your behalf
- Retrieve engagement and analytics data
- Access your profile information

Each connected platform has its own privacy policy:
- [Instagram Privacy Policy](https://help.instagram.com/519522125107875)
- [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- [YouTube Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [LinkedIn Privacy Policy](https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- [Pinterest Privacy Policy](https://policy.pinterest.com/privacy-policy)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: View all data associated with your account
2. **Export**: Download your content and analytics data
3. **Delete**: Permanently remove your account and all associated data
4. **Disconnect**: Remove any connected platform at any time
5. **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics collection and notifications

## Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. This action:

- Permanently removes all your content
- Deletes all analytics and platform connection data
- Removes your account credentials
- Cannot be undone

Account deletion completes within 24 hours.

## Children's Privacy

Creator Studio Lite is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Provide your information to data brokers

We MAY share data:
- When required by law
- To protect our legal rights
- With your explicit consent

## International Data Transfers

Your data is stored locally on your device. When you connect to third-party platforms, data transfers are governed by those platforms' policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:
- In-app notifications
- Email (if provided)
- Updated "Last Modified" date

## Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests:

**Email**: privacy@creatorstudio.app

For data deletion requests or privacy concerns, please include:
- Your account email
- Description of your request
- Any relevant details

We will respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
